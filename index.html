About Our Project

Empowering citizens, enabling better governance across every ward.

Our Mission

Bridging Citizens & Government

The Smart Civic Governance System is a digital platform that makes local governance transparent, accountable, and accessible to every citizen — from complaint filing to budget tracking.

  • 📝

    Submit Complaints

    Citizens report civic issues like road damage, garbage, streetlights, water leakage, and drainage problems.

  • 🔍

    Track in Real Time

    Get live updates on complaint status — pending, in progress, or resolved — with before/after photo proof.

  • 📊

    Monitor Spending

    View municipal budget allocations and actual expenditure across all departments transparently.

  • 🗳️

    Participate & Vote

    Join community polls, vote on local development decisions, and make your voice heard digitally.
